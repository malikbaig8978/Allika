About Allika EcoProducts

Allika is providing sustainable livelihoods and woman empowerment through apprenticeship training and further education. Using the beautiful but devastating weed, the water Hyacinth, they are removing the weed from ponds, lakes and clogged agricultural canals and harvesting all parts of the plant. The stem they dry and use for weaving, the other parts they create a natural fertiliser (weaving products are 98% organic). Removing the plant, creates health benefits as it clears the contaminated water and reduces the mosquito levels near communities, and allows an increased water flow for the farmers (also the government spends many billions removing the weed). Currently with a staff of 12, the growth projection is to increase to 10,000 livelihoods in the next 5 years, as the training of the women also includes them being trained up to train, not just in weaving, but communications, IT software and entrepreneurship so that they will be in direct competition with Allika. It is with this approach that the sustainable large scale growth that can be achieved.
